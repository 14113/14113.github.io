How I Work - and why

When coaching, I use basic coaching skills: open-ended questions, defining a SMART goal, not evaluating the client, notes for you. An integral part of my work is the use of other useful techniques: NLP, systematics, transformation coaching, and more ...

I am still learning in my field. I attend other useful courses, have my mentors and use supervision.

The first online meeting is free. We get to know each other, discuss expectations, agreements, find out if we understand each other.

I will ask about the topic you want to look at. If the topic is not completely clear, we can focus on your priorities.

When it comes to first encounters, we often focus on a long-term goal or vision. It is important that it is interesting and attractive enough for you. ↓

If we agree, I will offer you a total of six paid meetings once a month. Of course, you can interrupt it at any time. The main reason for just six meetings is to define the intention for a certain period of time.
