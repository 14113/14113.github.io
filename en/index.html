How I work — and why I do it this way

I believe that the key to good cooperation is based on constructive and regular communication. And therefore, before the start of every project, I like to discuss the idea or problem directly with the client. Through these personal interactions, we come to understand each other more, and whether or not the collaboration would serve us well.

If we decide to work together, it is necessary from the beginning to be clear on a few key points. We will arrange evaluations of the project, invoicing, and agree on the time, place and how the application will be put into operation.

First, we put together your requirements, what you expect from the website, who it is for, its purpose, etc.

Based on these requirements, I will prepare the necessary analysis and the system specification.

After that, we will select the requirements with the current highest priority at that time, which will be part of the first sprint. ↓

Provided everything satisfies your requirements, the web will be deployed to production and the web site maintenance is ready to begin.

After you have assessed the previous sprint, we can discuss any suggestions from you, and implement them in any further sprints.

As soon as I have fulfilled all requirements, the work is submitted. At that point everything is ready for testing.
